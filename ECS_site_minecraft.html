Minecraft

Minecraft is an open sandbox game originally created by Marcus Pearsson, and more recently purchased by Microsoft. The game spawns you in a randomly generated world with little no instruction. The player is only bound by their own creativity.

Minecraft holds a particulary special place in my heart. It was one of the first games I ever played, both with friends and by myself. Life changed rapidly over that time, but the game kept drawing me back. My skills have only been improving since 2014.
